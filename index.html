Consumer Behaviour & Demand Forecasting System

"A B2B platform that analyzes consumer behavior, forecasts demand, and provides actionable business intelligence for smarter decisions."

Problem Statement

Every day, retailers make thousands of stocking decisions - not knowing what customers will actually buy tomorrow. Some shelves overflow. Others remain empty.
This project builds an intelligent system that learns from consumer behaviour patterns to predict future demand — before the shelves do.

Market Research

The retail technology landscape includes ERP systems that manage transactions, supply chain platforms focused on logistics optimization, and standalone analytics tools. However, these systems are often complex, expensive, or disconnected from real-time checkout data.

As a result, many retailers, especially small and mid-scale businesses, lack an integrated solution that connects consumer purchase behavior directly to demand and stocking decisions.

Innovation

Enterprise platforms provide forecasting and analytics but are often complex and large-scale. This project integrates
1. consumer behavior analysis,
2. demand intelligence,
3. a foundation for automated recommendations within a simplified, end-to-end retail architecture.
The differentiation lies in combining these elements in a unified and scalable system, designed to mirror real-world retail workflows.

System Methodology

Dataset / Input

Olist e-commerce dataset (~100K orders), preprocessed to extract customer features, product categories, and sales history.

Model / Architecture

Database (MySQL), Backend (API logic), and Frontend (User Interface). Structured for scalable demand analysis integration.

Problem Validation & Build Status

On-Ground Insights
Manual record-keeping remains common in small retail stores, particularly during end-of-day reconciliation.
Inventory mismatches are often identified only during periodic stock audits.
Sales data is typically reviewed only at a basic daily total level, limiting deeper analysis.
Retailers rely heavily on experience-based forecasting rather than structured analytics.
Bookkeeping activities reduce time available for customer engagement and business growth.
Lack of structured expiry monitoring leads to delayed identification of expired or near-expiry stock.
System Modules & Execution Progress

Data Management Engine

Structured relational schema designed for transactional storage and analytical processing.

Transaction Logging Engine

Automated real-time capture of sales transactions with timestamped database persistence.

Inventory Synchronization Module

Dynamic stock updates triggered upon transaction confirmation to maintain real-time accuracy.

Analytics Dashboard Engine

Visualization layer enabling structured sales insights and trend monitoring.

Demand Forecasting Engine

Demand trend modeling under integration to enable predictive inventory planning.

Inventory Signal & Risk Monitoring Module

Translates forecast and sales trends into actionable inventory risk signals.

AI Recommendation Module

Planned advisory system for optimized product stocking and intelligent decision support.
